Call for Volunteers

BSides Barcelona is an Information Security Conference. We’re a volunteer organized event, put on by and for the community, and we truly strive to keep information free.

We need good, reliable folks, we have Volunteer opportunities on for load-in/set-up all the way through load-out/tear-down, the day of the conference. We have one team of Volunteers divided in 2 shifts of 6 hour each.

The opportunities are:

  • - Front desk: it’s entitled to perform check-in, sell BSidesBCN swags, provide general information to participants
  • - Room guard: it’s a stable, fixed presence in the room. It’s duty is to keep a watchful eye on our assets and participants
  • - Record man: Record the session
  • - Runners/Floaters: their responsibility is to be willing to perform any required, last minute, task
  • - Social Media Manager: their responsibility is to manage a dedicated social media account in order to reply to users, give status updates, be able to communicate effectively the event and its atmosphere

The only requirement for Volunteering, besides being 18 or older, is to perform the required job with a smile and a helping attitude.

In order to be a Volunteer we require a commitment to work a 6 hours. Bear in mind that we will try to keep the Volunteer schedule on time, but delay may happen so extra work time may be required. After your shift ends, you’re allowed to attend the conference as a participant. This way you can still be able to work your shifts and enjoy all of the conference. If you have questions before signing up please feel free to send an email to with the subject “Volunteering”.

All volunteers will receive a special volunteer t-shirts to be worn during your shifts. If you’re really willing to put in the time, we really want to reward you for your effort.
Come share your community spirit with the community. it’s a great way to meet new folks, make new friends and learn new skills! In addition to our staff, we need about 20 volunteers to make this wonderful event for around 200 participants happen. Be one of the few, the proud, the BSidesBCN Volunteer!
